Privacy Policy for The Function

Last updated: July 18, 2026

The Function ("we," "our," "the app") is a campus party-finder app. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and password. Your password is never stored in plain text — it's handled securely by our authentication provider, Supabase.

Location data. With your permission, we access your device's GPS location to show you how far away parties are and to help you drop a pin when posting a party. You can deny location access and still use the app; distances just won't be shown.

Content you post. When you post a party, we store the party name, location, optional flyer link, ticket link, cover price, description, accent color, and social media link you provide. If you choose to post anonymously, your account isn't attached to the listing publicly, though we retain an internal record of who created it for moderation purposes.

Activity data. We keep a record of which parties you mark as "going" and which you favorite, tied to your account, so the app can show your RSVPs and favorites list.

How We Use Information

We use the information above only to operate the app's core features: showing nearby parties, calculating distance, displaying crowd levels, and letting you track parties you're interested in. We do not sell your data, and we do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs of any kind.

Data Sharing

Party details you post (name, location, description, links, etc.) are visible to other users of the app, since that's the point of the app. We do not share your email address, precise location, or account activity with other users or third parties, except:

  • Supabase, our backend/database provider, which stores app data on our behalf.
  • Apple, for the native Maps feature on iOS (Apple Maps), which may independently collect location data per Apple's own privacy practices when the map is in use.

Data Retention

Party listings automatically expire and are removed 8 hours after posting. Account information, favorites, and RSVP history are retained until you delete your account.

Your Choices

You can deny location permission at any time in your device settings. To request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

The Function is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email us at jamesmo27@icloud.com.
